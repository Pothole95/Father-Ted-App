About the Show

Father Ted was a groundbreaking Irish sitcom that aired from 1995 to 1998. Created by Graham Linehan and Arthur Mathews, this iconic Irish comedy became a cultural phenomenon, winning multiple BAFTA awards and being voted the greatest Irish sitcom of all time in 2004.

Quick Facts

  • 25 episodes across 3 series
  • Filmed entirely on location in Ireland

  • The iconic parochial house was a real house in County Clare
